What This Means for Your Backlist

There is no automatic exemption for legacy ebooks.
If a title is still for sale, licensed, or downloadable in the EU, it is considered on the market and must comply.

In practical terms:

  • EPUB 2 files are at risk
  • Titles without semantic structure or accessibility metadata will fail future validation
  • Backlist titles will be the first to be de-prioritised or flagged by retailers and libraries

Enforcement will be incremental and uneven. Reduced discoverability, silent delisting, or rejection at the point of update is more likely than immediate penalties.

The EAA regulates the present, not the past.
