What You Should Do (Now)

Move to EPUB 3.3
Legacy EPUB 2 pipelines are obsolete for EU compliance.

Fix your XHTML
Use real structure, not visual styling. Headings, lists, and landmarks matter. Logical order matters.

Add accessibility metadata
Declare:

  • Conformance level
  • Accessibility features
  • Navigation features

Accuracy matters. Overclaiming matters too.

Audit existing titles
Backlist titles sold in the EU are included. Compliance is not only for new releases.

Include ONIX metadata
Ensure your distribution feeds declare accessibility so storefronts and libraries can act appropriately.

Document your process
If challenged, proof of intent and structured files matters.
